On making
"The craftsman does not impose form on material. The craftsman listens until the material speaks."
Yanagi Soetsu  ·  The Unknown Craftsman, 1972

Every object in this collection was made by a single hand. No division of labor. The same person who chose the clay threw the form, loaded the kiln, and decided when it was finished.

We do not make more than we can make well.
